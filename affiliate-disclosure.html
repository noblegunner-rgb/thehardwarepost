How The Hardware Post Makes Money

Last updated: April 2026

The Hardware Post is a free resource for homeowners and DIY buyers. We make money through affiliate marketing — when you click a link on our site and buy a product, we may earn a commission from the retailer. This comes at no additional cost to you.

Our Affiliate Partners

We participate in affiliate programs with the following retailers:

  • Amazon Associates — As an Amazon Associate, The Hardware Post earns from qualifying purchases.
  • Walmart Creator Program
  • Lowe's Creator Network
  • Home Depot Affiliate Program
  • Tractor Supply Affiliate Program

We may add or change partners over time.

What This Means for You

  • We earn a commission (typically 1%–6% depending on the product and retailer) when you purchase through our links.
  • We only recommend products we believe are genuinely worth buying.
  • Commission rates do not influence our product rankings or recommendations.
  • If we receive a free product for review, we will disclose that separately.
  • If a post is sponsored (paid by a brand for placement), we will clearly label it as "Sponsored."

How We Pick Products

Every product on this site goes through a scoring process that evaluates:

  • Current discount vs. historical price
  • Review count and rating quality
  • Seasonal relevance
  • Content suitability and practical use case
  • Merchant trust and shipping reliability
  • Return risk

Products that don't meet our quality bar don't get featured — regardless of commission rate.
