    How The Hardware Post works

    We're a small team of homeowners who got tired of searching through 47 "best of" lists that all recommend the same products without ever explaining why.

    The Hardware Post is simple: we research tools, gear, and home essentials, then give you a straight answer on what's worth buying and what to skip. Every product goes through a scoring process that looks at real review data, price history, and seasonal relevance — not commissions.

    We don't use affiliate links. The products we recommend link directly to the retailer — no tracking, no commissions, no incentive to push one brand over another. We recommend what we'd buy ourselves, full stop.
